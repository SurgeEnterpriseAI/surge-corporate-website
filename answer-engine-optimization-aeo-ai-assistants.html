Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) for AI Assistants

Get your brand cited inside AI-generated answers, not just search rankings

Home › AI & Innovation › Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) for AI Assistants

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of structuring your content so AI assistants can read it, trust it, and cite it directly in their answers. It matters because in 2026, 44% of AI search users say AI is their primary, preferred source of information. If an AI assistant doesn't cite you, your brand is effectively invisible. AEO focuses on factual content, structured data, and third-party validation rather than traditional keyword ranking.

What AEO is and why it matters

Answer Engine Optimization shifts the goal from ranking on a results page to being quoted inside an AI-generated answer. AI systems first filter out roughly 83% of candidate pages based on how accessible and well-structured they are, then extract answers from what survives. About 60% of Google searches are now 'zero-click,' meaning users get their answer from an AI overview without visiting any site. If you're not in that summary, the user likely never reaches you.

  • AI systems discard about 83% of candidate URLs before extracting answers, so technical structure is essential.
  • Third-party sources are cited 6.5 times more often than a brand's own domain, so off-site reputation matters.
  • A strong presence on Reddit and Quora makes a domain roughly 4x more likely to be cited; G2 and Trustpilot presence can raise citation chances 3x.
  • Visitors referred by AI assistants convert 3-6 times higher than other channels because the AI has already answered their questions.
