AI-Driven Personalization at Scale with Adobe AEP

Turn customer data into real-time, on-brand experiences with agentic AI on Adobe Experience Platform.

Home › AI & Innovation › AI-Driven Personalization at Scale with Adobe AEP

AI-driven personalization at scale with Adobe Experience Platform (AEP) means using autonomous AI agents to segment audiences, generate content, and orchestrate customer journeys in real time. In 2026, AEP's Agent Orchestrator manages specialized AI agents that work on their own to optimize and personalize experiences. Features like the Data Insights Agent, Computed Attributes, and Brand Concierge let marketers deliver relevant, on-brand content without heavy technical effort. Surge, a Premier Adobe Experience Cloud Partner, helps enterprises adopt and implement these capabilities.

What it is and why it matters

Personalization on Adobe Experience Platform has moved beyond simple predictions to 'agentic AI'—AI agents that act autonomously to do complex marketing work. The Agent Orchestrator manages these agents so they can optimize content, build audience segments, and personalize journeys in real time. This lowers the technical barrier for marketers and helps brands deliver relevant experiences faster, at a larger scale, while keeping content on-brand.

  • Agent Orchestrator manages AI agents that run autonomously to segment audiences and personalize journeys.
  • The Data Insights Agent lets marketers ask questions in plain language inside Customer Journey Analytics, no complex segment-building required.
  • Computed Attributes (May 2026) summarize event data into profile attributes you can activate in Real-Time CDP or Adobe Journey Optimizer.
  • Brand Intelligence and Brand Concierge agents build a knowledge base from approvals and campaigns to reduce off-brand content.
  • The Adobe–IBM watsonx integration helps manage predictive models with added trust and transparency.
  • A Kevel streaming destination connector activates AEP audiences for real-time retail media ad targeting.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is AI-driven personalization at scale with Adobe Experience Platform?

It's the use of autonomous AI agents in AEP to segment audiences, generate content, and orchestrate customer journeys in real time. The Agent Orchestrator manages these agents so brands can deliver relevant, on-brand experiences at large scale with less manual effort.

What is the AEP Agent Orchestrator?

The Agent Orchestrator is an Adobe Experience Platform capability that lets businesses manage AI agents which operate autonomously to optimize content, segment audiences, and personalize customer journeys in real time.

What are Computed Attributes in AEP?

Introduced in May 2026, Computed Attributes provide an intuitive interface to summarize event data into profile attributes. These attributes can then be activated in Real-Time CDP or Adobe Journey Optimizer for more relevant personalization.

How does Adobe keep AI-generated personalization on-brand?

Adobe's Brand Intelligence and Brand Concierge agents build a continuously updated knowledge base from approval decisions, campaigns, and collected data. This reduces the risk of publishing off-brand content while customizing layouts and content at scale.
